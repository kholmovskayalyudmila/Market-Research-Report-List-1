<p><h1>Vacuum Jacketed Pipe Market Size, Growth and Forecast from 2023 - 2030</h1></p><p><strong>Vacuum Jacketed Pipe Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>Vacuum Jacketed Pipe (VJP) is a type of pipe used for transporting cryogenic fluids such as liquefied natural gas (LNG) or liquid nitrogen. It consists of an inner pipe and an outer jacket, with the annular space between them evacuated to create a vacuum. This vacuum insulation greatly minimizes heat transfer and significantly reduces the boil-off rate of cryogenic fluids.</p><p>The Vacuum Jacketed Pipe Market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. One of the primary drivers of this growth is the increasing demand for cryogenic fluids in various industries, such as energy, healthcare, food and beverage, and electronics. The growth in these industries is driven by the need for cost-effective and efficient transportation and storage of cryogenic fluids.</p><p>Moreover, the expanding LNG infrastructure globally is further fueling the demand for vacuum jacketed pipes. With the rising focus on clean energy and the transition from conventional fuels to natural gas, there is a growing need for the transportation and distribution of LNG in an environmentally friendly manner. Vacuum jacketed pipes play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient transfer of LNG from the production site to end-users.</p><p>Another trend observed in the market is the increasing adoption of vacuum jacketed pipes for cryotherapy applications. Cryotherapy is gaining popularity as a medical treatment for various conditions and injuries. Vacuum jacketed pipes are used to transport the liquid nitrogen required for cryotherapy, providing precise temperature control and minimizing the risk of heat transfer.</p><p>In conclusion, the Vacuum Jacketed Pipe Market is projected to experience significant growth due to the rising demand for cryogenic fluids and the expansion of LNG infrastructure. Additionally, the adoption of vacuum jacketed pipes for cryotherapy applications is expected to further contribute to market growth. The market is estimated to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.</p></p>
