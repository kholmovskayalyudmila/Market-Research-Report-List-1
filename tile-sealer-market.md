<p><ul><li>Penetrating Sealants</li><li>Surface Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tile sealer is a protective coating used to enhance and preserve the appearance of tiles. There are two main types of tile sealer: penetrating sealants and surface sealants. Penetrating sealants deeply penetrate the tile pores to create a protective barrier from within, preventing stains and moisture absorption. Surface sealants, on the other hand, create a thin film on the surface of the tile to protect against stains and add shine. Both types provide different levels of protection and have their own applications in the tile sealer market.</p></p>
