<p><strong>The Fuel Antidetonant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MMT</li><li>MTBE</li><li>TAME</li><li>TBA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel antidetonant market consists of various types such as MMT, MTBE, TAME, and TBA. MMT (Methylcyclopentadienyl Manganese Tricarbonyl) is an additive that enhances octane rating, reducing engine knocking in gasoline. MTBE (Methyl Tertiary Butyl Ether) is a widely used oxygenate that increases gasoline's oxygen content, aiding in cleaner combustion. TAME (Tertiary Amyl Methyl Ether) is another oxygenate that improves fuel efficiency and reduces emissions. TBA (Tertiary Butyl Alcohol) is an alcohol-based gasoline additive that helps prevent engine knocking. These types serve as essential components in fuel formulations to optimize engine performance and comply with emission standards.</p></p>
