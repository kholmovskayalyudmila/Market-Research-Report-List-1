<p><p>Hydraulic tubing refers to the pipes used in hydraulic systems to transmit fluids and power. Spiral wire hydraulic tubing is a type of tubing that is constructed with multiple layers of steel wire, which provides high strength and resistance to pressure. On the other hand, wire braided hydraulic tubing consists of a single layer of steel wire braided around the tube, offering flexibility and protection against abrasion. Both types are commonly used in various industries for their durability and ability to handle high-pressure applications.</p></p>
