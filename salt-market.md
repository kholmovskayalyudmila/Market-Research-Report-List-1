<p><p>The salt market can be categorized into three main types: sea salt, well mineral salt, and lake salt. Sea salt is obtained by evaporating seawater, retaining its natural minerals and trace elements. Well mineral salt is sourced from underground mines, where it is extracted and naturally dried, preserving its essential minerals. Lake salt is derived from saltwater lakes and undergoes a process of solar evaporation. All these varieties of salt offer different flavors, textures, and mineral content, catering to individual preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>
