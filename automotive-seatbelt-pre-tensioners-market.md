<p><p>Automotive seatbelt pre-tensioners refer to safety mechanisms that instantly tighten a vehicle's seatbelt during a collision or sudden braking, ensuring a secure fit on the occupants. Buckle pre-tensioners are integrated into the seatbelt buckle, automatically retracting the belt to remove slack. On the other hand, retractor pre-tensioners are placed in the seatbelt retractor, pulling the belt taut. Apart from these two common types, there are also other pre-tensioner variations in the market that aim to enhance occupant protection and improve overall safety in automobiles.</p></p>
