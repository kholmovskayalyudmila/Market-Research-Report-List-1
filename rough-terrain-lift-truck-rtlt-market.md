<p><p>The Rough Terrain Lift Truck (RTLT) market is divided into two main types - Telescopic RTLT and Masted RTLT. Telescopic RTLTs have a boom arm that extends and retracts, allowing for increased reach and versatility. They are ideal for applications requiring a higher lifting capacity and extended reach. On the other hand, Masted RTLTs feature a vertical mast that elevates the load and are commonly used for indoor and outdoor material handling tasks with lower lifting heights. Both types cater to different needs, providing efficient solutions for various industries.</p></p>
