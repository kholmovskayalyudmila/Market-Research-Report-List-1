<p><strong>The Color Mark Photoelectric Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-beam Sensors</li><li>Retro-reflective Sensors</li><li>Diffuse-reflective Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color mark photoelectric sensors are used in various industries for detecting the presence or absence of colored marks on objects. Through-beam sensors consist of a transmitter and a receiver, where the beam of light is interrupted by the mark. Retro-reflective sensors use a reflector to bounce the light back to the receiver after hitting the mark. Diffuse-reflective sensors emit light and receive the reflected light from the mark. Each type has its own advantages and applications, providing reliable color mark detection for different industrial needs.</p></p>
