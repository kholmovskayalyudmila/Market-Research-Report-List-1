<p><ul><li>Organic Electrolyte</li><li>Inorganic Electrolyte</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-solid battery market is divided into two main types: the organic electrolyte market and the inorganic electrolyte market. Organic electrolyte batteries utilize non-aqueous solvents based on materials such as carbonates, ethers, or esters. On the other hand, inorganic electrolyte batteries employ solid-state ionic materials, like oxides or sulfides, as conductive materials. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, which include factors such as cost, energy density, safety, and cycle life. The choice of electrolyte heavily influences the performance and characteristics of semi-solid batteries.</p></p>
