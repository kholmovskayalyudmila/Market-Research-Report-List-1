<p><p>The market for replacement rear view mirrors can be classified into two types: CCD camera and CMOS camera. CCD cameras use an image sensor that converts light into electrical signals, providing high-quality images with good color reproduction. CMOS cameras, on the other hand, use a different technology that allows for lower power consumption and a smaller form factor. Both types of cameras offer improved visibility and safety while reversing a vehicle. However, CCD cameras are known for their better image quality, while CMOS cameras offer advantages in terms of cost and size.</p></p>
