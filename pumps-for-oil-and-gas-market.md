<p><strong>The Pumps for Oil and Gas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The pumps for the oil and gas market can be classified into two main types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. </p><p>Centrifugal pumps work by using an impeller to create centrifugal force, which then pushes the fluid through the pump. They are widely used in the industry due to their high flow rate capability, but their efficiency decreases as the viscosity of the fluid increases.</p><p>Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, use mechanical means to push the fluid forward. They provide a constant flow rate regardless of the viscosity and are commonly used for high-pressure applications. However, they can be more expensive and require careful maintenance.</p></p>
