<p><p>The automotive heater blower market can be categorized into three types: down-blast discharge blower, horizontal discharge blower, and up-blast discharge blower. A down-blast discharge blower is designed to blow air downwards and is commonly used in passenger vehicles. Horizontal discharge blowers are designed to blow air horizontally and are typically used in commercial vehicles. Conversely, up-blast discharge blowers are designed to blow air upwards and are commonly used in larger vehicles such as buses or trucks. Different types cater to specific vehicle requirements, ensuring efficient heating and air circulation.</p></p>
