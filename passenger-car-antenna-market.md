<p><p>The passenger car antenna market can be categorized into different types such as fin type, rod type, screen type, and others. Fin type antennas are sleek and compact, usually mounted on the vehicle's roof and provide excellent performance. Rod type antennas are long and thin, commonly mounted on the fender or trunk of the car, providing good reception. Screen type antennas are integrated into the windshield or rear window, offering a discreet design. Lastly, other types of antennas may include shark fin antennas, retractable antennas, or hidden antennas designed for specific vehicle models.</p></p>
