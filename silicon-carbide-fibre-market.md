<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Fibre market is divided into two main types: Continuous/Long Fibre and Chopped/Short Fibre. These fibres offer excellent strength and stiffness, making them suitable for applications requiring high-performance reinforcements. On the other hand, Chopped/Short Fibre refers to silicon carbide fibres that are cut into shorter lengths, which are commonly used as fillers or additives in various composites, providing enhanced mechanical properties and improving overall material performance.</p></p>
