<p><p>Animal feed components are ingredients and additives used in the formulation of animal feed. These alternatives not only provide adequate nutrition to animals but also address the challenges related to conventional protein sources, such as limited availability and high production costs.</p><p>Furthermore, the use of feed additives as animal feed components is gaining traction in the market.
