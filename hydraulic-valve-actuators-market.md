<p><strong>The Hydraulic Valve Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Actuator</li><li>Rotary Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic valve actuators market comprises two main types: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators are designed to move in a straight line, delivering linear motion to the valve disc or stem. They are commonly used in applications requiring linear movement, such as gate valves. On the other hand, rotary actuators are built to rotate the valve disc or stem, providing rotary motion to control the flow of fluids. They find widespread use in applications such as ball valves and butterfly valves, where rotational movement is required.</p></p>
