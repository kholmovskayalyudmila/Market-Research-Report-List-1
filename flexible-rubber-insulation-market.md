<p><p>The flexible rubber insulation market is categorized into four types: NBR based, EPDM based, chloroprene based, and others. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based insulation is known for its excellent resistance to oils and chemicals, making it ideal for industrial applications. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based insulation is popular for its exceptional weather resistance, making it suitable for outdoor applications. Chloroprene based insulation offers good flame retardancy properties, making it preferred for fire-safety applications. The "others" category includes rubber insulation materials that do not fall into the aforementioned types but still provide notable insulation properties.</p></p>
