<p><p>Oleochemical fatty acids market can be categorized into two main types: unsaturated fatty acids and saturated fatty acids. Unsaturated fatty acids are characterized by the presence of double bonds in their carbon chain, making them liquid at room temperature. They are commonly used in various applications such as cosmetics, food processing, and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, saturated fatty acids do not have any double bonds, resulting in a solid state at room temperature. They have applications in soap manufacturing, lubricants, and animal feed.</p></p>
