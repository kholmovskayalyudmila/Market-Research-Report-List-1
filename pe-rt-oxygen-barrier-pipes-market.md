<p><p>PE-RT (Polyethylene of Raised Temperature Resistance) Oxygen Barrier Pipes have two market types - PE-RT Ⅰ and PE-RT Ⅱ. PE-RT Ⅰ pipes have a lower resistance to high temperatures and are suitable for use in underfloor heating systems, while PE-RT Ⅱ pipes have a higher resistance to high temperatures and are suitable for use in more demanding applications such as radiant floor heating or radiator connections. Both types of pipes have an oxygen barrier which prevents the entry of oxygen, ensuring longevity and reliability in heating systems.</p></p>
