<p><p>CVD epitaxy equipment is used in the manufacturing of semiconductor devices and thin films. The market is categorized into two main types: MOCVD equipment and other CVD epitaxy equipment. MOCVD (Metal-organic Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment utilizes a process where metal-organic compounds are vaporized to grow thin films with high precision and uniformity. On the other hand, other CVD epitaxy equipment includes various deposition techniques such as atmospheric pressure CVD, low-pressure CVD, and plasma-enhanced CVD. Both types are crucial in the production of advanced electronic components.</p></p>
