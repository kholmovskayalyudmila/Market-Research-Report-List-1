<p><p>The gaseous air freshener market consists of different types, including ozone and negative ion products. Ozone air fresheners release ozone molecules, which can eliminate odors by oxidizing them. Negative ion air fresheners, on the other hand, release negatively charged ions, which attach to positively charged particles in the air, causing them to fall and reduce airborne odors. Both types aim to improve indoor air quality and eliminate unpleasant smells by using different mechanisms of action.</p></p>
