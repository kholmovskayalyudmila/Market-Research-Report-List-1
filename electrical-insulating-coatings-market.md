<p><p>Electrical Insulating Coatings are used to provide protection against electrical currents and prevent the flow of electricity. There are different types of coatings available in the market. Epoxy Coatings, known for their high thermal resistance and excellent adhesion, offer effective insulation properties. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) Coatings provide low friction and excellent resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. Ceramic Coatings offer high dielectric strength and thermal stability.
