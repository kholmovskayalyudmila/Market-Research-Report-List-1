<p><strong>The TypeC PD Control Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UFP</li><li>DFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TypeC PD Control Chip market can be broadly categorized into two types: UFP and DFP. UFP, which stands for Upstream Facing Port, refers to the TypeC Port that acts as a power receiver, enabling devices to draw power from a power source. On the other hand, DFP, or Downstream Facing Port, refers to the TypeC Port that acts as a power provider, allowing devices to supply power to connected devices. Understanding these categories is crucial for manufacturers and consumers in selecting the appropriate TypeC PD Control Chip for their specific power transfer needs.</p></p>
