<p><p>Automotive paint protection films are available in different types such as PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) type, PU (Polyurethane) type, TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) type, and others. PVC type films offer excellent protection against scratches and contaminants, but may lack self-healing properties. PU type films provide good optical clarity and self-healing capabilities, ensuring long-lasting protection. TPU type films are known for their high durability and resistance to yellowing over time. Other types may include hybrid films or specialized variants that cater to specific customer requirements, offering unique features or benefits.</p></p>
