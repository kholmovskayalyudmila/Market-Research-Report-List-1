<p><strong>The Snow Melting Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HydronicSnowmeltSystems</li><li>ElectricSnowmeltSystems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Snow melting controllers market includes two main types - hydronic snowmelt systems and electric snowmelt systems. Hydronic snowmelt systems are operated using hot water or a mixture of water and antifreeze that circulates through a network of pipes under the pavement. These systems are highly efficient but require complex installation. Electric snowmelt systems, on the other hand, utilize electric heating cables embedded in the pavement to melt snow and ice. They are easier to install but may incur higher operating costs. Both types of systems provide effective snow and ice removal for improved safety and convenience.</p></p>
