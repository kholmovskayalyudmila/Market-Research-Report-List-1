<p><p>Wood pellet fuel market is divided into two types: white pellet market and black pellet market. White pellets are made from clean and dry wood residue, such as sawdust or wood shavings. They are compressed under high pressure without any additives and have a light color. White pellets are widely used in residential heating and are known for their high energy efficiency and low emissions. On the other hand, black pellets are made from torrefied wood, a process that involves heating the biomass in the absence of oxygen. Black pellets have a darker color and higher energy density, making them suitable for industrial and large-scale power generation applications.</p></p>
