<p><p>Snowmelting cables market types can be classified as constant temperature and temperature-limited. Constant temperature cables maintain a consistent and continuous heat to melt snow, regardless of the surrounding temperature. On the other hand, temperature-limited cables regulate the heat output based on the external temperature, preventing overheating and optimizing energy consumption. These market types cater to different needs and preferences, allowing users to choose the most suitable option for their snowmelting requirements.</p></p>
