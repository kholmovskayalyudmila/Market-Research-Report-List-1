<p><p>Flexible laminations adhesives are used in various industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics. Water-based adhesives, as the name suggests, are solvent-free and have low VOC content, making them environmentally friendly. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing quick evaporation and high bonding strength. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and become liquid when heated, offering fast bonding and good adhesion to various substrates. Solventless adhesives are 100% solid systems, curing through chemical reactions, providing excellent adhesion, and high strength while being eco-friendly.</p></p>
