<p><p>Proppants are materials used in the oil and gas industry to support fractures and enhance the production of hydrocarbons during hydraulic fracturing. There are three main types of proppants: frac sand proppants, resin-coated proppants, and ceramic proppants. Frac sand proppants are made from high-purity sand grains and are the most widely used proppants due to their cost-effectiveness. Resin-coated proppants are coated with a resin material to improve their performance and allow for better flow control. Ceramic proppants are made from ceramic materials and are used in high-pressure and high-temperature environments due to their superior strength and conductivity.</p></p>
