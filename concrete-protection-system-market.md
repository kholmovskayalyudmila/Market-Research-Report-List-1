<p><p>The concrete protection system market consists of various types, including hydrophobic impregnations, reinforcement corrosion protection, protective coatings, and the concrete repair market. Hydrophobic impregnations are substances used to make concrete water repellent and protect it from damage caused by water absorption. Reinforcement corrosion protection involves the application of coatings or cathodic protection to prevent corrosion of steel reinforcement within the concrete. Protective coatings are applied to concrete surfaces to provide an extra layer of protection against various environmental factors. The concrete repair market involves the repair and restoration of damaged or deteriorated concrete structures.</p></p>
