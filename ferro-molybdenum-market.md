<p><p>Ferro-molybdenum is an alloy composed of iron and molybdenum, with molybdenum content ranging from 60% to 70%. Ferro-molybdenum is particularly used in the manufacturing of stainless and high-strength steel, which finds applications in industries such as automotive, construction, aerospace, and oil & gas.</p><p>The global ferro-molybdenum market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The aerospace and automotive industries, in particular, are adopting lighter materials for improved fuel efficiency and reduced carbon emissions.</p><p>The ongoing research and development activities to enhance the properties of ferro-molybdenum alloys, such as increased hardness and wear-resistance, also contribute to the market growth.</p><p>Overall, the ferro-molybdenum market is projected to experience significant growth, with a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 14.4% during the forecast period.
