<p><p>The nut harvester market is categorized into three main types: self-propelled, trailed, and mounted nut harvesters. Self-propelled nut harvesters are machines that can move independently and are equipped with harvesting mechanisms. Trailed nut harvesters are towed behind a vehicle and are operated by the movement of the towing vehicle. Mounted nut harvesters, on the other hand, are attached to a vehicle or a tractor and work in synchronization with its movement. These different types of nut harvesters offer various options for farmers and agricultural businesses to efficiently collect nuts during harvest.</p></p>
