<p><p>Architectural signage can be classified into two main types: exterior signage and interior signage. Exterior signage refers to signs that are installed on the outside of buildings, such as building identification signs, directional signs, and wayfinding signs. These signs help visitors and customers locate entrances, navigate through the building complex, and find specific locations. On the other hand, interior signage is installed inside buildings to guide people within the premises, including room names, floor numbers, and exit signs. Both exterior and interior signage play a crucial role in providing clear communication, enhancing brand visibility, and improving overall user experience.</p></p>
