<p><p>Refrigeration oil is a special type of lubricant formulated for use in refrigeration systems. Synthetic oils offer better lubrication and improved performance in extreme temperature conditions. They are also more stable and have a longer lifespan compared to mineral oils. This trend can be attributed to the growing awareness regarding the benefits of synthetic oils among end-users.</p><p>Another trend impacting the market is the increasing adoption of environmentally-friendly refrigeration oils. <p><ul><li>Mineral Oil</li><li>Synthetic Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigeration oil market types can be classified into mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil and is commonly used in refrigeration systems due to its low cost and good lubricating properties. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is produced through chemical processes and offers enhanced lubricity, better thermal stability, and reduced oil consumption. Despite being more expensive than mineral oil, synthetic oil is widely used in applications where higher performance and extended oil life are required, such as in high-pressure refrigeration systems.</p></p>
