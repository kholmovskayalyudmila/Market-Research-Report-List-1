<p><p>Composite materials are materials made from two or more different types of materials, resulting in improved properties compared to their individual components. The polymer matrix composite market utilizes polymers such as carbon fiber reinforced plastics, providing lightweight, strong and corrosion-resistant materials. Metal matrix composites combine metal with reinforcing particles like ceramic or carbon fibers, enhancing their strength, stiffness, and thermal conductivity. Ceramic matrix composites utilize ceramic fibers in a ceramic matrix, leading to materials with exceptional high-temperature resistance, low thermal expansion, and excellent mechanical properties. These types of composites find applications in aerospace, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
