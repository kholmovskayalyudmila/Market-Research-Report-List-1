<p><strong>The Automatic Transfer Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open transition</li><li>Closed transition</li><li>Static transfer switch (STS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic transfer switches (ATS) are devices used to automatically transfer power supply from one source to another in case of a power outage. The market for ATS can be categorized into different types, including open transition, closed transition, static transfer switch (STS), and others. Open transition refers to a momentary loss of power during transfer, while closed transition ensures a seamless transfer without any interruption. STS offers continuous power by instantly transferring between sources. The "others" category includes various types such as delayed transition or overlap. These different types cater to different requirements and needs in power transfer scenarios.</p></p>
