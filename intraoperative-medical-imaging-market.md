<p><p>The intraoperative medical imaging market includes various types of imaging technologies used during surgeries. Intraoperative computed tomography (CT) provides 3D images of the patient's anatomy, aiding precise navigation during surgery. Intraoperative ultrasound involves real-time imaging using sound waves, allowing surgeons to visualize structures within the body. Intraoperative magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) utilizes a magnetic field to generate detailed images during surgery. Intraoperative mobile C-arms are portable X-ray machines that provide real-time imaging guidance during procedures. These technologies enhance surgical accuracy and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
