<p><p>The dye mordant market can be divided into two main types: natural and synthesis. Natural mordants are derived from organic sources such as plants, minerals, and animals. They offer a wider range of color options and are often more cost-effective. Both types of mordants play a crucial role in dyeing processes by helping the dye bond to the textile fibers, resulting in vibrant and long-lasting colors.</p></p>
