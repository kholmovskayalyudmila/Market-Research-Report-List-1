<p><strong>The Casing Advance System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Casing</li><li>Retrievable Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Casing advance systems are employed during drilling operations to securely advance the casing string into the drilled hole. The market for these systems can be broadly classified into two types: permanent casing and retrievable casing. Permanent casing involves installation of the casing string with the intention of leaving it in place permanently, providing essential support and sealing the wellbore. In contrast, retrievable casing is designed for temporary placement and removal, allowing for easier access to the wellbore for maintenance and interventions. These different casing options cater to varying drilling requirements and operational needs in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
