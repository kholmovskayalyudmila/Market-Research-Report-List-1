<p><p>The raised floor market consists of two types: steel plate and calcium sulfate board. Steel plate raised floors are made of metal sheets with galvanized finishes, providing durability and excellent load-bearing capacity. They are ideally used in areas with heavy equipment or machinery. On the other hand, calcium sulfate board raised floors are composed of gypsum calcium sulfate panels, offering fire resistance and vibration damping properties. These types are commonly utilized in data centers, cleanrooms, and buildings requiring enhanced safety measures.</p></p>
