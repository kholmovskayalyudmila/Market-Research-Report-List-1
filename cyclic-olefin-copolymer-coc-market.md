It is known for its excellent transparency, heat resistance, chemical resistance, and low moisture absorption. On the other hand, Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) is a similar material but with a homopolymeric structure, offering similar properties but with higher glass transition temperature and better dimensional stability. Both COC and COP have a significant market presence and are chosen based on specific requirements in different industries.</p></p>
