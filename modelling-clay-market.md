<p><ul><li>Non-Dry Clay</li><li>Air-Dry Clay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modelling clay market consists of two main types: non-dry clay and air-dry clay. Non-dry clay retains its pliability and moisture, making it suitable for longer-term projects and intricate details. It requires baking or firing to harden. On the other hand, air-dry clay does not require any additional processes for hardening and dries naturally when exposed to air. This type is often preferred for quick projects or for those who do not have access to baking or firing equipment. Both types offer different conveniences and advantages depending on the artist's needs.</p></p>
