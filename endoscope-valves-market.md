<p><strong>The Endoscope Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disposable</li><li>Reusable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope valves market can be categorized into two types: disposable and reusable valves. Disposable valves are designed for single-use and are discarded after each procedure. These valves offer convenience as they eliminate the need for cleaning and sterilization, reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Reusable valves, on the other hand, are designed to be sterilized and reused for multiple procedures. They offer cost-effectiveness in the long run but require proper cleaning and maintenance to ensure safety and effectiveness. Both types of valves play a crucial role in endoscopy procedures, providing different advantages based on specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
