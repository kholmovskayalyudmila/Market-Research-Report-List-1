<p><p>The cold chain transport vehicle market primarily consists of two types: refrigerated trailers and refrigerated trucks. Refrigerated trailers are large containers that are hauled by trucks or tractor-trailers, providing a mobile cold storage solution for transporting perishable goods, such as food and pharmaceuticals, at controlled temperatures. On the other hand, refrigerated trucks are standalone vehicles that have built-in refrigeration units, allowing for the transportation of perishable goods independently. Both options play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and safety of temperature-sensitive products during transportation.</p></p>
