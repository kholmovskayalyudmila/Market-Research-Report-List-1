<p><p>Cold room panels are specialized panels that are used for the construction of cold rooms or walk-in refrigerators. Growing consumer awareness regarding the quality and safety of stored products is driving the demand for cold room panels. Additionally, strict regulations pertaining to the storage and transportation of refrigerated products are also fueling the market growth.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the cold room panels market is segmented based on type, application, and region. Based on type, the market can be categorized into three main types: polyurethane (PUR) panels, polyisocyanurate (PIR) panels, and mineral wool panels. Among these, PUR panels are the most widely used due to their excellent insulation properties.</p><p>On the basis of application, the market is segmented into food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, logistics, and others. The food and beverage industry has the largest market share, owing to the increasing demand for perishable food items and the need for proper cold storage facilities.</p><p>According to the latest trends, there is a growing demand for energy-efficient cold room panels that help reduce energy consumption.
