<p><p>The self-compacting concrete market can be divided into three types: powder type, viscosity agent type, and combination type. Powder type self-compacting concrete is composed of cement, fine aggregate, and mineral additives, providing high flowability without segregation. Viscosity agent type self-compacting concrete consists of cement, fine aggregate, mineral additives, and viscosity agents, which increase the viscosity and improve stability during transportation. Combination type self-compacting concrete is a combination of powder and viscosity agent types, offering both the flowability of powder type and the stability of viscosity agent type self-compacting concrete.</p></p>
