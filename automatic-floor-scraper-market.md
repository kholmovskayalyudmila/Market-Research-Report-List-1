<p><p>The automatic floor scraper market consists of three types of machines: walk-behind, ride-on, and self-propelled. Walk-behind machines are operated by a person pushing the equipment manually. Ride-on machines allow the operator to sit on the machine while it is being driven, providing more comfort and increased productivity. Self-propelled machines are motorized and operate without the need for manual assistance. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences, offering efficiency and versatility in floor scraping applications.</p></p>
