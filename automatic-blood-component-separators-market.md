<p><p>The Automatic Blood Component Separators market offers two types of systems: Single Separation Path and Double Separation Path. In Single Separation Path systems, blood is processed once, resulting in the production of one desired blood component. This system is suitable for situations requiring only one specific component. On the other hand, Double Separation Path systems allow blood to be processed twice, enabling the production of two different blood components. This system is ideal for applications where multiple components are needed from a single blood donation.</p></p>
