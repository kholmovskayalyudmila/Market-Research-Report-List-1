<p><ul><li>Three Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof laminate flooring is classified into two main types: three-layer and multilayer market. In the three-layer market, the flooring is composed of three layers: a bottom layer made of solid material, a core layer made of wood or fiberboard, and a top layer coated with a wear-resistant material. On the other hand, the multilayer market consists of flooring with several layers, including a built-in moisture barrier and an underlayment. Both types offer excellent protection against water damage and are popular choices for areas prone to moisture, such as bathrooms and kitchens.</p></p>
