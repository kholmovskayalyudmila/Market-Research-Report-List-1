PTFE type heat seal tapes are known for their high temperature resistance and low friction properties. TPU type heat seal tapes exhibit excellent flexibility and durability, making them suitable for various applications. PI type heat seal tapes offer exceptional thermal stability and chemical resistance. These different types cater to diverse industries and provide unique benefits according to their specific material composition and characteristics.</p></p>
