<p><p>SUV wheel market can be categorized into three types: casting, forging, and other markets. Casting refers to the process of pouring molten metal into a mold, which allows for complex designs and lower production costs. Forging entails shaping heated metal using compression forces, resulting in strong and durable wheels. Lastly, the other market includes alternative wheel manufacturing methods, such as flow forming or rotary forging, which offer unique benefits like enhanced strength, reduced weight, or improved performance. These different market types cater to the varied needs and preferences of SUV owners.</p></p>
