<p><p>Antiscalant market is mainly divided into four types: Phosphonates, Carboxylates or Acrylic, Sulfonates, and Others. Phosphonates are chemicals used to inhibit scale formation by forming a protective layer on surfaces. Carboxylates or Acrylic are organic compounds that prevent scales by controlling crystal growth and preventing crystal adhesion. Sulfonates are surfactants that disrupt scale formation by preventing crystal nucleation. The "Others" category includes various chemicals with antiscalant properties that may utilize different mechanisms to inhibit scale formation. These types of antiscalant market solutions cater to diverse industrial requirements for scale prevention.</p></p>
