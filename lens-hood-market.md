<p><p>Lens hoods are accessories used in photography to reduce glare and lens flare, ultimately improving image quality. There are different types of lens hoods available in the market. Cylindrical lens hoods have a simple cylindrical shape and are generally used with prime lenses. Taper lens hoods have a wider mounting area that tapers towards the front, suitable for wide-angle lenses. Petal lens hoods have a unique shape resembling a flower and are commonly used with zoom lenses. Square lens hoods have a square design, adaptable for lenses with a circular or rectangular front element.</p></p>
