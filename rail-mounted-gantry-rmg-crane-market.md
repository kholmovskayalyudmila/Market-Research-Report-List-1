<p><p>A Rail Mounted Gantry (RMG) Crane is a type of crane that is mounted on rails and primarily used in ports and terminals for moving and stacking containers. It is also known as a container handling gantry crane. RMG cranes are equipped with a trolley system and rubber tires, allowing them to move horizontally and vertically along the rails to efficiently handle containers. These cranes are mostly automated, utilizing advanced technologies such as sensors and computer control systems to improve productivity and accuracy.</p><p>The future outlook for the Rail Mounted Gantry (RMG) Crane Market is promising.
