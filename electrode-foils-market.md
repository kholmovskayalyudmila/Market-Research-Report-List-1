<p><p>The electrode foils market is divided into two main types: the cathode foil and the anode foil. The cathode foil is a negatively charged electrode used in various applications such as batteries and capacitors. It plays a crucial role in the electron flow during discharge. On the other hand, the anode foil is a positively charged electrode that attracts and stores electrons during charging. Both foils are essential components in electrochemical devices and are key in determining the overall performance and efficiency of these devices.</p></p>
