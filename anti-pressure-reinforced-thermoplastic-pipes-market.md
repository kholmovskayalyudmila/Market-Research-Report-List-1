<p><p>Anti-pressure reinforced thermoplastic pipes are a type of piping system used for various applications. Non-metallic pipes are made of polymer materials, offering corrosion resistance, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. Metallic pipes, on the other hand, consist of a reinforcing layer made of steel wire or other materials, providing additional strength and durability. Both types have their advantages and are used in different industries, depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
