<p><p>The Hemodialysis (HD) membrane market offers various types, including Unmodified Cellulose, Modified/Regenerated Cellulose, and Synthetic membranes. Unmodified Cellulose membranes are made from natural cellulose fibers and provide efficient small molecule clearance. Modified/Regenerated Cellulose membranes are created by modifying or regenerating cellulose to enhance biocompatibility and reduce clotting. Synthetic membranes are artificially manufactured using polymer materials, enabling precise control of pore size and permeability to ensure high filtration efficiency in hemodialysis. These different membrane types cater to diverse needs, allowing for effective and customized treatment options in hemodialysis therapy.</p></p>
