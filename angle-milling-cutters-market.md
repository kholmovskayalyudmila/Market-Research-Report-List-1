<p><p>Angle milling cutters are a type of cutting tool used in machining processes to produce angled surfaces or slots on workpieces. Single angle milling cutters are designed with a single cutting edge and are used to create an angle on one side of the workpiece. Double angle milling cutters have two cutting edges and are commonly used to machine V-shaped slots or grooves. Other types of angle milling cutters include shell end mills, which have a cylindrical shape with cutting teeth on the periphery, and dovetail milling cutters, which are used to create dovetail-shaped cuts.</p></p>
