There are three main types: photoimageable solder resist ink, thermal curable solder resist ink, and UV curable solder resist ink. Photoimageable solder resist ink is developed using photolithography, creating intricate patterns. Thermal curable solder resist ink is heated to form a protective layer. UV curable solder resist ink hardens when exposed to ultraviolet light. Each type offers specific advantages, providing options for different manufacturing needs and processes.</p></p>
