<p><p>Microelectronic soldering solder pastes are used in the manufacturing and repair of electronic components and circuit boards. There are three types of solder pastes in the market: water-soluble solder paste, no-clean solder paste, and others. Water-soluble solder paste can be easily cleaned with water after soldering, making it suitable for applications where residue removal is necessary. No-clean solder paste leaves minimal residue after soldering and doesn't require cleaning. The 'others' category includes specialized solder pastes designed for specific applications, such as lead-free solder paste or low-temperature solder paste.</p></p>
