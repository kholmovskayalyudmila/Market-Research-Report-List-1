<p><p>The dental curing lamp market consists of two main types: LED and Halogen. LED curing lamps utilize light-emitting diodes to emit a specific wavelength of light that activates dental curing materials to harden or set. LED lamps are known for their longer lifespan, energy efficiency, and decreased heat output. On the other hand, Halogen curing lamps use Halogen bulbs to emit a broader spectrum of light for curing dental materials. They are less expensive but require regular bulb replacements and have a higher heat output.</p></p>
