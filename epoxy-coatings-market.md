<p><strong>The Epoxy Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powder-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy coatings are commonly used for protecting or enhancing surfaces. Solvent borne epoxy coatings are formulated with volatile solvents that evaporate during the curing process, creating a strong and durable protective layer. Waterborne epoxy coatings use water as a dispersion medium instead of solvents, making them environmentally friendly. Powder-based epoxy coatings are applied electrostatically as dry powder and then heated to form a tough coating. These types of coatings provide excellent adhesion and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
