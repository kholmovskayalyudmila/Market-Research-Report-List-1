<p><strong>The Retread Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure Process</li><li>Mold Cure Process</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The retread tires market is divided into two main types: the pre-cure process and the mold cure process. In the pre-cure process, a new tread is applied to a tire casing using a pre-manufactured, pre-vulcanized tread strip. The tread is bonded to the casing through a chemical bonding process. On the other hand, the mold cure process involves applying uncured rubber to the tire casing, which is then placed in a mold where the tread pattern is formed and cured under high pressure and heat. These different processes cater to the specific needs and preferences of various customers.</p></p>
