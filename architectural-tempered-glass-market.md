<p><strong>The Architectural Tempered Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tempered Glass</li><li>Bent Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Architectural tempered glass is categorized into two main types: flat tempered glass and bent tempered glass. Flat tempered glass is produced by heating and cooling a glass sheet uniformly, resulting in increased strength and durability. It is widely used in windows, doors, and other architectural applications. On the other hand, bent tempered glass is shaped by heating the glass sheet and bending it into curved or irregular forms. This type of glass is commonly used in architectural designs that require unique shapes or curved installations. Both types of tempered glass offer enhanced safety and are resistant to thermal stress.</p></p>
