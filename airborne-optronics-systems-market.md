<p><p>The airborne optronics systems market includes two major types: multispectral and hyperspectral systems. Multispectral systems use sensors that capture information from specific wavelength bands within the electromagnetic spectrum. This enables the detection and analysis of different objects based on their unique spectral signatures. Hyperspectral systems, on the other hand, split the electromagnetic spectrum into numerous narrow bands, allowing for more detailed and precise spectral data collection. These systems are widely used in various applications such as agriculture, environmental monitoring, defense, and surveillance.</p></p>
