<p><p>The Automotive Camera Active Alignment Station market includes different types of systems based on degrees of freedom (DOF). 4 DOF systems offer four types of movement or adjustment options to align camera modules, such as pitch, yaw, roll, and lateral translation. 5 DOF systems provide an additional movement option, typically vertical translation. 6 DOF systems offer all the movement options available in 4 and 5 DOF systems, along with an added degree of freedom, usually longitudinal translation. These different DOF options allow for precise calibration and alignment of automotive camera modules.</p></p>
