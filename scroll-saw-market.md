<p><ul><li>C-arm</li><li>parallel arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scroll saw market consists of two main types: C-arm and parallel arm. C-arm scroll saws are designed with a C-shaped arm that connects the blade to the base, offering enhanced stability and support during cutting. They are commonly used in professional woodworking workshops, emphasizing precision and intricate cutting. On the other hand, parallel arm scroll saws have a blade connected to two parallel arms, which allows for greater control and flexibility in handling curves and tight corners. They are more popular among hobbyists and DIY enthusiasts. Both types cater to different user preferences and requirements within the woodworking industry.</p></p>
