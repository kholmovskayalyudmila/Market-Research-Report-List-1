<p><p>Ultrasound units are medical devices used for diagnostic imaging purposes. The market is classified into three types: external ultrasound units, internal ultrasound units, and endoscopic ultrasound units. External ultrasound units are used on the surface of the body to examine organs and structures. Internal ultrasound units are inserted into body cavities to examine organs like the uterus or bladder. Endoscopic ultrasound units are used during endoscopic procedures to examine the gastrointestinal tract. These different types of ultrasound units cater to various medical needs and provide valuable imaging information for diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
