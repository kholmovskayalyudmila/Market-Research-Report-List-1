<p><p>Silicon-based fingerprint sensors are commonly divided into two market types: swipe sensors and area sensors. Swipe sensors require the user to swipe their finger across the sensor's surface, capturing the fingerprint image during the motion. These sensors are typically found in devices like smartphones and laptops. On the other hand, area sensors allow the user to place their finger on the sensor's surface without any movement, capturing the fingerprint image instantly. These sensors are commonly used in applications that require higher security, such as access control systems or government institutions.</p></p>
