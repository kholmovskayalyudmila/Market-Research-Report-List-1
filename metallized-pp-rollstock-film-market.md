<p><p>Metallized PP rollstock film refers to a type of packaging material made from polypropylene (PP) that has been coated with a thin layer of metal (usually aluminum) through a vacuum metallization process. This metallized layer provides the film with enhanced barrier properties, including increased resistance to moisture, oxygen, and light, which makes it ideal for various applications such as food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and industrial goods.</p><p>The market analysis of the metallized PP rollstock film industry shows promising growth prospects.
