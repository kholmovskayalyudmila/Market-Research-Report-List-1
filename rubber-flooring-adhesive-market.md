<p><p>Rubber flooring adhesive market is classified into three types: epoxy adhesive, latex adhesive, and acrylic adhesive. Epoxy adhesive is known for its strong bonding capabilities, making it suitable for heavy traffic areas. Latex adhesive, on the other hand, offers good moisture resistance and flexibility, making it ideal for installing rubber flooring in wet areas. Acrylic adhesive provides good environmental resistance and ease of application. Each adhesive type has its own unique qualities, catering to different installation requirements in the rubber flooring industry.</p></p>
