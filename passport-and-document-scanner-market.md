<p><strong>The Passport and Document Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>Barcode</li><li>OCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passport and document scanner market can be categorized into three types: RFID, Barcode, and OCR. RFID scanners use radio frequency identification technology to read and collect information from RFID-enabled documents or passports. Barcode scanners, on the other hand, use optical scanning to capture and interpret barcode data displayed on documents. OCR (Optical Character Recognition) scanners convert printed or handwritten text into digital data for easy storage and retrieval. Each type of scanner offers its own unique benefits and serves specific needs in the market.</p></p>
