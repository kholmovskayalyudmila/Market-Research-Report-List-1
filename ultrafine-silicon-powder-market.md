<p><p>The ultrafine silicon powder market is categorized into three types: densified silicon powder, semi-densified silicon powder, and undensified silicon powder. Densified silicon powder undergoes a process that increases its density, resulting in improved flowability and compaction properties. Semi-densified silicon powder has lower density than densified powder but higher than undensified powder, offering intermediate performance characteristics. Undensified silicon powder has the lowest density among the three types, indicating reduced flowability and compaction properties. These variations in density enable different applications and performance properties, catering to diverse market requirements.</p></p>
