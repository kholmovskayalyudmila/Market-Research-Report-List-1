<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel injection pump market consists primarily of two types: common rail fuel injection pump and rotary fuel injection pump. Common rail fuel injection pump is a high-pressure pump that delivers fuel to the injectors at a constant pressure, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. On the other hand, rotary fuel injection pump uses a rotating mechanism to control fuel delivery, providing a continuous and uniform fuel supply. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing engine performance and are widely used in modern automotive systems.</p></p>
