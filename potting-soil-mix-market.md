<p><p>Potting soil mix market types include soilless mixes, composts, gravel, inert media, and others. Soilless mixes are made from a combination of materials like peat moss, vermiculite, and perlite. Composts are created from decomposed organic matter to provide nutrients to plants. Gravel is used in soil mixes to improve drainage and prevent waterlogging. Inert media, such as sand, are used in hydroponic systems where plants are grown without soil. Lastly, "others" refer to any additional materials or blends that are used in potting soil mixes, such as coconut coir or bark.</p></p>
