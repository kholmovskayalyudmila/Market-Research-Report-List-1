<p><p>Aluminium pigments are widely used in various industries for their ability to enhance the appearance and properties of products. Leafing aluminium pigments have flakes that tend to align parallel to the surface, creating a metallic and reflective effect. These pigments are ideal for applications where a high degree of gloss is desired. On the other hand, non-leafing aluminium pigments have flakes that remain in a random orientation, resulting in a duller appearance. They are commonly used in applications where hiding power and opacity are important.</p></p>
