<p><p>Off-Circuit Tap-Changers are devices used in electricity transmission systems to vary the voltage of power transformers. They are available in four market types. Off-Circuit Disk Tap-Changers consist of a series of metal contacts arranged in disks and are mainly used in low-voltage applications. Off-Circuit Bar Tap-Changers use a series of bars to connect different coil taps, making them suitable for medium to high-voltage systems. Off-Circuit Drum Tap-Changers have a rotating drum mechanism and are commonly used in high-voltage applications. Off-Circuit Cage Tap-Changers feature a rotating cage-like structure and are suitable for very high voltage levels.</p></p>
