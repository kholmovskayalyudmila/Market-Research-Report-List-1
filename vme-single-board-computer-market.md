<p><p>VME Single Board Computer (SBC) refers to a type of computer system that is built on the VME (Versa Module Eurocard) bus standard. VME SBCs are widely used in applications that demand high reliability, ruggedness, and long product lifecycles, such as aerospace, defense, industrial automation, and transportation.</p><p>The future outlook of the VME SBC market is expected to be positive.
