<p><p>Mouth cancer, also known as oral cancer, refers to the uncontrollable growth of cells in the oral cavity. Treatment options for mouth cancer depend on the stage and location of the cancer, as well as the patient's overall health. The commonly used treatment modalities for mouth cancer include surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and immunotherapy. Surgery is often the primary treatment and may involve tumor removal, lymph node dissection, or reconstructive surgery.
