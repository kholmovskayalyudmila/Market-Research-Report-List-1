<p><p>Refrigerating machine oils can be classified into two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from petroleum and is commonly used in standard refrigeration systems. It is cost-effective and provides good lubrication to the machines. On the other hand, synthetic oil is artificially made from chemical compounds and offers enhanced performance and durability. Synthetic oils are often preferred for high-pressure applications or in extreme temperature conditions where mineral oil may not be suitable.</p></p>
