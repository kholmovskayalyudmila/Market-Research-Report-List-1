<p><p>The dairy enzymes market consists of various types of enzymes used in the dairy industry. Lactase is an enzyme that helps in the breakdown of lactose, making it suitable for individuals with lactose intolerance. Chymosin is an enzyme that is used in cheese production to coagulate milk. Microbial rennet is a type of rennet derived from microbial sources, which is used in cheese making. Lipase is an enzyme that aids in the cheese ripening process by breaking down fats. Overall, these enzymes play crucial roles in improving dairy product quality and catering to specific consumer needs.</p></p>
