<p><p>The sink drain market offers various types of options, including pop-up, lift, and pull out drains. Pop-up drains are commonly seen in traditional sinks, where a stopper can be pushed down to close the drain and pushed again to open it. Lift drains operate similarly, but instead of being pushed, a lever is used to open and close the drain. Pull out drains are typically found in modern sink designs, allowing users to easily remove the drain for cleaning or maintenance purposes.</p></p>
