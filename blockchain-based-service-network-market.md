<p><p>Blockchain-based Service Network (BSN) offers two types of market in the context of permissioned and permissionless blockchains. Permissionless blockchains, like Bitcoin and Ethereum, enable anyone to participate without restrictions, allowing for decentralized and transparent transactions. On the other hand, permissioned blockchains require users to gain authorization, making them suitable for private networks and restricted access. Both types offer advantages such as immutability, security, and efficiency, but permissioned blockchains are more suitable for businesses or organizations seeking controlled and collaborative environments.</p></p>
