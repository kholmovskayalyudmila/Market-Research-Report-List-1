<p><p>High-cis polybutadiene rubber is a type of synthetic rubber that is made through the polymerization of butadiene. It has a high cis content, meaning that a significant portion of the butadiene molecules in the rubber have a cis configuration. The increasing demand for high-performance rubber materials in various industries, such as automotive, construction, and industrial, is a major factor driving the growth of this market.
