<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The vinyl composition flooring (VCF) market is segmented into two types: single layer and multilayer. Single layer VCF consists of a single vinyl layer and is relatively inexpensive and easy to install. It is commonly used in low-budget applications where durability is not a major concern. On the other hand, multilayer VCF is made up of multiple layers including a wear layer, a printed design layer, and a backing layer. This type of flooring offers superior durability, resilience, and aesthetic appeal, making it more suitable for high-traffic and commercial applications.</p></p>
