<p><p>The C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test is a blood test that measures the levels of C-reactive protein in the body. The test is primarily used to detect and monitor the presence of inflammation and to diagnose and monitor conditions such as infections, autoimmune diseases, and certain types of cancer.</p><p>The future outlook of the CRP Test Market is promising, with a projected growth rate of 11.2% during the forecasted period.
