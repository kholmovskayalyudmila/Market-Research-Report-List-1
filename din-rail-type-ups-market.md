<p><p>DIN Rail Type UPS refers to uninterruptible power supply systems that are designed to be mounted on DIN rails. There are two main types in the market: DIN Rail DC UPS and DIN Rail AC UPS. DIN Rail DC UPS provides backup power in direct current (DC) form, allowing devices to continue operating during power outages. On the other hand, DIN Rail AC UPS offers backup power in alternating current (AC) form, ensuring continuity of electrical supply for devices connected to it. Both types of DIN Rail UPS are commonly used in industrial automation and control applications.</p></p>
