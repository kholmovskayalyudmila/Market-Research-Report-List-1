<p><p>Concrete anchors/fasteners are essential in construction and engineering projects to securely fix objects to concrete surfaces. Mechanical anchors/fasteners rely on physical interlocking mechanisms to provide stability, such as expansion anchors, sleeve anchors, and wedge anchors. On the other hand, adhesive anchors/fasteners utilize chemical adhesives to bond the anchor into the concrete, using two-part epoxies or injectable resins. Both types offer reliable and durable solutions, with mechanical anchors providing versatility for various load capacities and adhesive anchors providing ease of installation on smooth surfaces and higher load capacities.</p></p>
