<p><p>Filtration fabrics market can be categorized into different types based on their weaving patterns. Monolayer weave refers to a single-layered fabric that provides basic filtration functions. Double weave fabrics consist of two layers, offering enhanced filtration performance. Three-layer weave fabrics have an additional layer, providing even better filtration efficiency. Apart from these, there are various other types of filtration fabrics available in the market, each serving specific filtration requirements. These include multilayer, needle felt, and mesh fabrics, among others. These different fabric types cater to diverse industries such as chemical, pharmaceutical, food processing, and wastewater treatment, ensuring effective filtration solutions.</p></p>
