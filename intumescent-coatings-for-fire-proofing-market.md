<p><p>Intumescent coatings for fireproofing are specialized coatings applied to surfaces to provide passive fire protection. The market for these coatings can be categorized into two types: one-component coatings and multicomponent coatings. </p><p>One-component coatings are pre-mixed and ready to use, requiring no additional additives or mixing. They offer ease of application and are suitable for smaller projects or where simplicity is desired. </p><p>Multicomponent coatings, on the other hand, consist of separate components that need to be mixed together before application. These coatings offer greater flexibility and are often used in larger projects or when specialized performance characteristics are required.</p></p>
