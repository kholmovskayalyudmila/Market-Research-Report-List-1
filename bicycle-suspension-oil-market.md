<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The bicycle suspension oil market consists of two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from petroleum and is commonly used in lower-end suspension systems. It is affordable but requires more frequent maintenance due to its lower viscosity. On the other hand, synthetic oil is chemically engineered and has superior performance, making it suitable for high-end suspension systems. Although synthetic oil is more expensive, it provides better stability, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance needs.</p></p>
