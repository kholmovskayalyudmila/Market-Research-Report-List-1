<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive EPS (Electric Power Steering) motor market includes various types of motors. CEPS motors (Column-Assist Electric Power Steering) are installed in the steering column and can provide steering assistance. REPS motors (Rack Electric Power Steering) are placed on the steering rack and assist in steering movements. PEPS motors (Pinion-Assist Electric Power Steering) are situated on the pinion gear to offer steering assistance. EHPS motors (Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering) combine electric and hydraulic systems for enhanced steering control. These motor types play a crucial role in improving steering performance and enhancing the overall driving experience.</p></p>
