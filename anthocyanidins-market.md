There are several types of anthocyanidins including delphinidin, cyanidin, petunidin, peonidin, and others. Delphinidin produces a blue color, cyanidin creates red to purple hues, petunidin imparts purple to black colors, and peonidin contributes to red and purple pigments. These different anthocyanidin types have various applications in the food, beverage, and cosmetic industries due to their natural coloring properties and potential health benefits.</p></p>
