<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Mechanical turbocharging is a type of turbocharger for commercial vehicles that uses a mechanical connection between the engine and the turbocharger to boost engine performance. This type of turbocharging relies on mechanical energy to compress the air entering the engine, resulting in increased power output. </p><p>Exhaust turbocharging, on the other hand, utilizes the exhaust gases generated by the engine to drive the turbocharger. These gases pass through the exhaust turbine, spinning it and subsequently driving the compressor, which compresses the air entering the engine. This compressed air improves combustion efficiency, leading to enhanced engine power and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
