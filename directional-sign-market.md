<p><p>Directional sign market types can be categorized into wall-mounted and floor-standing signs. Wall-mounted signs are typically fixed on walls or vertical surfaces and provide clear guidance to people in a specific direction. These signs are commonly found in buildings, airports, and shopping malls. On the other hand, floor-standing signs are freestanding structures placed on the ground. They are larger and more visible, often used in outdoor areas such as parking lots or events to direct people to specific locations. Both wall-mounted and floor-standing signs enhance navigation and ensure efficient movement of individuals.</p></p>
