<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber-Reinforced Carbon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li><li>Liquid Impregnation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber-reinforced carbon (CFRC) market consists of two major types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process. CVD involves the process of heating a carbon source gas to form carbon atoms on a substrate, resulting in the growth of carbon fiber. It is commonly used to produce CFRC with high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, the liquid impregnation process includes soaking carbon fibers in a liquid carbon precursor, followed by heating and carbonization. This method is efficient in producing CFRC with good mechanical properties and can be used for various applications.</p></p>
