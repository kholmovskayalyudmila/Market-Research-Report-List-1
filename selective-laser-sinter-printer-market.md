<p><p>The selective laser sintering (SLS) printer market can be categorized into two types: benchtop SLS printers and industry-grade SLS printers. Benchtop SLS printers are compact, affordable, and suitable for individuals or small-scale businesses. They offer limited build volumes and may have lower precision and accuracy. On the other hand, industry-grade SLS printers are larger, more expensive, and designed for industrial use. They offer larger build volumes, higher precision, and can handle more demanding applications. Industry-grade SLS printers are typically used by larger companies for rapid prototyping and manufacturing purposes.</p></p>
