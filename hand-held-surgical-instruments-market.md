<p><p>Hand-held surgical instruments are essential tools used by surgeons during surgical procedures. Forceps are used for delicate tissue handling and grasping objects, while retractors help surgeons visualize and access the surgical site. Dilators are used to enlarge body passages or structures, allowing instruments to be inserted. Graspers are designed to hold organs or tissues securely during surgery. Scalpels are sharp cutting instruments used to make precise incisions. These instruments are vital in ensuring successful surgeries and are widely used in various medical specialties.</p></p>
