<p><p>Ingeo Fiber is available in two market types: Solution Spinning and Melt Spinning. Solution Spinning refers to the process where a solution of Ingeo resin is made and then spun into fibers. This method is often used to produce fine, uniform fibers with high strength. On the other hand, Melt Spinning involves melting the Ingeo resin and then spinning it into fibers. This process is suitable for creating thicker fibers and is commonly used in applications such as carpeting or geotextiles. Both market types offer different characteristics and applications for Ingeo Fiber.</p></p>
