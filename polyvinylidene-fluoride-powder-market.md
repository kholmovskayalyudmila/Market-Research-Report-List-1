<p><p>The Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) Powder market is divided into two main types, namely Homopolymer and Copolymer. Homopolymer refers to PVDF powder made solely from the monomer vinylidene fluoride. This type of PVDF powder offers excellent chemical resistance, high UV stability, and good mechanical strength. On the other hand, Copolymer PVDF powder is produced by incorporating small amounts of comonomers during the polymerization process. This type offers enhanced flexibility and better processability, making it suitable for applications that require moldability and ease of fabrication.</p></p>
