<p><p>Briquettes are a popular type of fuel used worldwide, and the market offers various types to cater to different needs. Solid cylindrical briquettes are compact and have a hole at the center, making them suitable for stoves or boilers. RUF briquettes are produced using mechanical pressure, resulting in a block shape with holes throughout, ensuring good air circulation for effective burning. Pini-Kay briquettes are known for their unique shape with chamfered edges, providing a longer burning time and higher heat output. These different types cater to specific requirements and provide options for consumers in the briquette market.</p></p>
