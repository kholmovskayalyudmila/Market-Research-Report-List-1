<p><p>In-vehicle routers for public safety are available in two market types: cellular connection and wireless connection. </p><p>1. Cellular connection routers utilize the existing cellular network infrastructure to provide internet connectivity in emergency vehicles. These routers use SIM cards and operate on cellular data networks, enabling seamless and reliable connectivity even in remote areas.</p><p>2. Wireless connection routers, on the other hand, leverage wireless technologies such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth to establish internet connectivity within the vehicle. These routers create a local network, allowing devices inside the vehicle to connect and access the internet without relying on a cellular network.</p></p>
