<p><p>The residential portable generators market consists of two main types - AC Generators and DC Generators. AC generators produce alternating current, which is the electric energy typically used in homes. These generators are suitable for powering household appliances and devices. On the other hand, DC generators produce direct current, which is commonly used in battery-operated devices. These generators are suitable for charging batteries or powering devices that specifically require DC power. Both AC and DC generators play a crucial role in providing power backup solutions for residential settings.</p></p>
