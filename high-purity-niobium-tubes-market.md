<p><p>High purity niobium tubes are categorized based on their purity levels, specifically as 3N and 4N. The term "N" represents the number of nines used to express the purity percentage of the metal. In this case, 3N denotes 99.9% purity and 4N corresponds to 99.99% purity. The higher the number of nines, the greater the purity of niobium. These classifications are essential for various industrial applications that require extremely pure niobium, such as in the production of superconductors, electrical components, and medical devices.</p></p>
