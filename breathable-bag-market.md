<p><p>The breathable bag market consists of two main types: micro perforation and macro perforation bags. Micro perforation bags are designed with tiny holes that allow for a controlled exchange of air, preventing spoilage and maintaining freshness of fruits and vegetables. These bags are commonly used in the agriculture and food industry. On the other hand, macro perforation bags have larger holes and are used for products that require more ventilation, such as textiles and industrial goods. Both types serve the purpose of providing breathability and preserving product quality.</p></p>
