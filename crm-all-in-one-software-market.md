<p><p>CRM All-in-One software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CRM software is hosted on the internet and allows users to access and manage their customer data remotely. It offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for hardware or infrastructure.
