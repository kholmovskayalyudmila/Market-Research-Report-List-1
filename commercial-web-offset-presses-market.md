<p><p>Commercial web offset presses are divided into different market types based on their speed capabilities. The market types include 16000 CPH (copies per hour), 30000 CPH, 36000 CPH, and others. These figures represent the number of copies that can be printed by the press within an hour. For example, a press with a speed of 16000 CPH can produce 16,000 copies in one hour. Similarly, presses with higher speeds like 30000 CPH or 36000 CPH can produce 30,000 or 36,000 copies respectively. The "others" market type is used to refer to presses with speeds outside of these specific ranges.</p></p>
