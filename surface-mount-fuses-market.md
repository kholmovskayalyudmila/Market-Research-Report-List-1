<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Mount Fuses market is divided into Type I and Type II. Type I fuses are designed to protect low-power circuits, such as smaller electronic devices, from overload and short circuits. They have lower voltage and current ratings, making them suitable for applications with lesser power requirements. On the other hand, Type II fuses are built for high-power circuits, like industrial equipment or appliances. They can handle higher voltage and current levels, providing robust protection against overloads and short circuits in more demanding applications. Both fuse types cater to distinct power needs in various electronic and industrial settings.</p></p>
