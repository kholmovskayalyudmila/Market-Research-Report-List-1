<p><p>Non-Toxic PU Catalysts, also known as non-toxic polyurethane catalysts, are substances that are used to initiate or accelerate the chemical reaction between polyols and isocyanates in the production of polyurethane (PU) foams, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Unlike conventional catalysts, non-toxic PU catalysts do not contain harmful substances such as mercury or formaldehyde, making them more environmentally-friendly and safer to handle.</p><p>The global Non-Toxic PU Catalysts Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The growing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable materials in various end-use industries such as construction, automotive, furniture, and packaging is a major driving factor for the market. By type, the market can be categorized into amine catalysts, organo-metallic catalysts, and others. Based on application, the market is divided into polyurethane foams, coatings, adhesives, sealants, and others.</p><p>Geographically, North America and Europe are the dominant regions in the Non-Toxic PU Catalysts Market, primarily driven by strict environmental regulations and high consumer awareness.
