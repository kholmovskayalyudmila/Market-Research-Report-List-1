<p><p>Multi-axis systems refer to the technology used for precision motion control in various industries. Positioning systems are a type of multi-axis system that accurately control the position of an object in multiple directions. These systems are commonly used in manufacturing, robotics, and automation industries. On the other hand, precision cross systems are another type of multi-axis system that enable precise movements in both linear and rotational directions.
