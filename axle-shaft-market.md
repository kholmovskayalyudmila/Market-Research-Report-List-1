<p><strong>The Axle Shaft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Propeller Shaft</li><li>Half Shaft</li><li>Axle Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axle shaft market consists of three primary types: propeller shaft, half shaft, and axle shaft. A propeller shaft connects the transmission to the rear axle, transmitting power from the engine to the wheels. A half shaft is used in front-wheel drive vehicles to transfer power from the transmission to the front wheels. Lastly, an axle shaft is found in rear-wheel drive vehicles and connects the differential to the rear wheels, transmitting power and supporting the vehicle's weight. These components play crucial roles in ensuring efficient power transfer and smooth vehicle operation.</p></p>
