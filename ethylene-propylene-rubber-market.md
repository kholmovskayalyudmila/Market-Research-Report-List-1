<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Rubber (EPM) and Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) are two types of rubber commonly used in various industries. EPM is a copolymer of ethylene and propylene, known for its excellent resistance to heat, ozone, and weathering. EPDM, on the other hand, is a terpolymer consisting of ethylene, propylene, and a diene monomer. It offers additional properties such as improved resistance to chemicals, aging, abrasion, and flexibility. The EPDM market encompasses a wide range of applications, including automotive, construction, electrical, and electronics industries.</p></p>
