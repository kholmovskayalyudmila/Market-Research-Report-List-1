<p><strong>The Absorbent Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Adherent</li><li>Adherent</li><li>Low-Adherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absorbent dressings market includes three major types: non-adherent, adherent, and low-adherent dressings. Non-adherent dressings are designed to minimize pain during removal and prevent damage to newly formed tissues. Adherent dressings are intended to stick to the wound bed, promoting effective wound healing and preventing leakage. On the other hand, low-adherent dressings strike a balance between non-adherent and adherent dressings, providing easy removal while still adhering to the wound bed. These different types cater to various wound types and patient needs in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
