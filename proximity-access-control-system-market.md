<p><p>A proximity access control system is a security solution that allows authorized individuals to gain entry to a building or specific areas within a building using a proximity credential such as a key card or fob. It relies on radio frequency identification (RFID) technology to communicate with the credential and grant access.</p><p>The future outlook of the proximity access control system market looks promising. The increasing need for secure access control systems, driven by rising security concerns across various industries such as corporate offices, healthcare facilities, and government organizations, is expected to drive the market growth. The adoption of these systems provides numerous benefits such as enhanced security, improved operational efficiency, and reduced administration costs.</p><p>The current outlook of the market is also positive. The advancements in RFID technology, along with the increasing awareness about the benefits of proximity access control systems, are fueling the market growth. Additionally, the integration of cloud-based solutions and mobile credentials offer convenience and flexibility, further driving the market demand.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a growing trend towards the adoption of contactless access control systems due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. These systems eliminate the need for physical contact with access devices, reducing the risk of virus transmission. This trend is expected to boost the market growth.</p><p>Overall, the proximity access control system market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.7% during the forecast period.</p></p>

This type is commonly employed in public areas like museums or parks, where unrestricted access is preferred.</p><p>On the other hand, Mandatory Access systems enforce strict regulations for entry and exit, requiring individuals to go through a verification process, such as biometric authentication or card scanning.
