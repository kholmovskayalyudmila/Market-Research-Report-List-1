<p><p>The Ingot Crane market consists of two main types: Permanent and Mobile. A Permanent Ingot Crane is fixed in a specific location, typically installed in steel mills or foundries, providing continuous handling of ingots. On the other hand, a Mobile Ingot Crane is designed with mobility in mind, allowing it to be easily transported and utilized at different sites. Mobile cranes are commonly used in construction projects or areas where the need for portability is essential. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently lifting and moving heavy ingots, but differ in terms of permanence and mobility.</p></p>
