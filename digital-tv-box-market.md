<p><p>A digital TV box, also known as a set-top box or a digital converter box, is an electronic device that allows an analog television to receive and display digital TV signals. The increasing availability of digital content and the rising popularity of streaming services are also contributing factors to the growth of the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the digital TV box market is benefiting from technological advancements such as the integration of streaming capabilities, smart features, and internet connectivity in the devices.
