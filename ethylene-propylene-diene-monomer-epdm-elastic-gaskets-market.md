<p><p>The Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM) Elastic Gaskets market includes various types of gaskets, such as Saddle and Waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets are designed in a shape resembling a saddle, which provides a tight and secure seal between two surfaces. They are commonly used in piping systems. On the other hand, Waveform gaskets have a wavy structure that allows them to adapt and conform to irregular surfaces, ensuring better sealing performance. These gaskets are widely used in applications where irregular or non-standard flange connections are present.</p></p>
