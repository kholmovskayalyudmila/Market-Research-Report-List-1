Sugar esters can be classified into three types based on the number of fatty acid groups attached to the sugar molecule. Monoesters have one fatty acid group, diesters have two, and triesters have three. Each type offers specific characteristics and benefits, making them suitable for different applications, such as food and beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
