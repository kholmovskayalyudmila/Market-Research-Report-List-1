<p><strong>The Aluminum Alloy Wheels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum alloy wheels market is divided into two major types: casting and forging. Casting involves pouring molten aluminum into a mold to create the wheel. This method is commonly used for mass production due to its low cost and high production rate. On the other hand, forging involves shaping aluminum through intense heat and pressure. This results in a stronger and more durable wheel, making it preferred for high-performance vehicles. While casting offers cost efficiency, forging provides enhanced performance capabilities.</p></p>
