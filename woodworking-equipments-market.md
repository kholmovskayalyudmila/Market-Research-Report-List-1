<p><p>Woodworking equipment market can be divided into two main types: power machinery and hand tools. Power machinery refers to equipment that is powered by electricity or fuel, such as table saws, planers, and jointers. These machines are highly efficient and can handle heavy-duty tasks. On the other hand, hand tools are non-powered tools that are manipulated manually, such as chisels, hand saws, and planes. Hand tools are typically used for detailed and precise woodworking tasks. Both power machinery and hand tools play a crucial role in the woodworking industry, catering to different needs and preferences of woodworkers.</p></p>
