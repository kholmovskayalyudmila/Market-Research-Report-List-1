<p><ul><li>Lithium-based Powder</li><li>Sodium-based Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cathode powders market is categorized into two main types: lithium-based powder and sodium-based powder. Lithium-based powder refers to a type of cathode powder that contains lithium as the key component. It is commonly used in rechargeable lithium-ion batteries due to its high energy density and stability. On the other hand, sodium-based powder consists of sodium as the main ingredient and is utilized in sodium-ion batteries. These batteries are considered a potential alternative to lithium-ion batteries, offering similar performance but with the advantage of using more abundant and cost-effective sodium resources.</p></p>
