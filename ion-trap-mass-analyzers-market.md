<p><p>Ion trap mass analyzers are used in mass spectrometry to measure the mass-to-charge ratio of ions. The market for ion trap mass analyzers is categorized into three types. Quadrupole ion trap mass analyzers use four electrodes to trap ions, allowing selective mass filtering and fragmentation. Linear ion trap mass analyzers use two electrodes to trap ions in a linear fashion, enabling higher ion capacity and greater sensitivity. Other types of ion trap mass analyzers include cylindrical, orbitrap, and quadrupole ion trap hybrid instruments, which combine features of multiple types for comprehensive analysis.</p></p>
