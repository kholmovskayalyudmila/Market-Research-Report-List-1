<p><p>The Fluoro Compounds market can be categorized into two main types: Organofluorine Compounds and Inorganic Fluorine Compounds. Organofluorine Compounds are a type of fluorocarbon compounds that contain carbon-fluorine bonds, which provide exceptional chemical and thermal stability. These compounds are commonly used in various industrial applications, including pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and polymers. On the other hand, Inorganic Fluorine Compounds are compounds that do not contain carbon-fluorine bonds. They are primarily used in industries such as aluminum production, uranium enrichment, and as refrigerants due to their unique properties.</p></p>
