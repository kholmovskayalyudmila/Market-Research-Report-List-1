<p><p>OTN (Optical Transport Network) Hardware Market includes three main types: OTN Switching, OTN Transport, and Optical Packet Platform Systems (P-OTS). OTN Switching refers to the switching of data in OTN networks, ensuring efficient and scalable transport. OTN Transport involves the transmission of data over long distances using optical fibers, providing high capacity and low latency connectivity. P-OTS systems combine the benefits of OTN and packet switching technologies, allowing the efficient transport of both circuit-switched and packet-switched data over the same network infrastructure.</p></p>
