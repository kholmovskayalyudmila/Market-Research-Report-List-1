<p><p>The three-phase circuit breaker market consists of two types: manual reset and automatic reset. In a manual reset circuit breaker, once it trips and interrupts the flow of electricity, it requires manual intervention to be reset and resume normal operation. These different types cater to different needs and preferences in terms of convenience and safety in the electrical circuit protection field.</p></p>
