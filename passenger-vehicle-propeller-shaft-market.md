<p><p>The passenger vehicle propeller shaft market consists of two main types: rigid shaft and hollow shaft. A rigid shaft is a solid and robust component used to transmit torque from the transmission to the rear differential of a vehicle. It offers high strength and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, a hollow shaft is a lightweight and flexible component with a hollow interior. It provides improved fuel efficiency and reduced vehicle weight while maintaining adequate strength for normal operating conditions. Both types vary in design and are chosen based on specific vehicle requirements.</p></p>
