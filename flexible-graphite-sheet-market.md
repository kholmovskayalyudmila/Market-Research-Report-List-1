<p><p>Flexible graphite sheet, also known as compressed graphite sheet or expanded graphite sheet, is a type of sheet made from high-quality natural graphite flakes. The market is primarily driven by increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance materials in industries such as automotive and aerospace. <p><p>The flexible graphite sheet market consists of two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural flexible graphite sheets are made from raw graphite, which has natural flake structures that allow it to easily conform to surfaces. These sheets have excellent thermal conductivity and are widely used in various industries. On the other hand, synthetic flexible graphite sheets are produced from organic materials, such as carbon fibers or expanded graphite, and offer improved mechanical properties and stability. Both types have their unique advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
