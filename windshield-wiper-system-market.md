<p><p>The Windshield Wiper System market is divided into two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM refers to wiper systems that are manufactured and installed by the original vehicle manufacturer. These systems are specifically designed to fit the particular vehicle model and meet the required quality standards. Aftermarket wiper systems, on the other hand, are produced by third-party manufacturers and are available for purchase as replacement parts. These systems provide an alternative option for vehicle owners to replace their wipers, often at a lower cost than OEM options.</p></p>
