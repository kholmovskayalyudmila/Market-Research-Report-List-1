<p><p>Polyalkylene Glycol (PAG) refers to a class of synthetic fluids that are derived from the polymerization of alkylene oxides such as propylene oxide or ethylene oxide. The growing demand for PAG from the automotive industry is one of the key factors driving the market growth. Additionally, PAG fluids also provide improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions, further contributing to their adoption in the automotive sector.</p><p>Another emerging trend in the polyalkylene glycol market is the increasing usage of PAG as base fluids in industrial lubricants.
