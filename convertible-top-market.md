<p><p>The convertible top market can be classified into two main types: hard tops and soft tops. Hard tops are made of rigid materials such as steel or aluminum, providing a solid roof structure when closed. They offer better insulation, noise reduction, and security. Soft tops, on the other hand, are made of fabric or vinyl and can be easily folded or retracted. They are typically lighter, more affordable, and provide a classic convertible look. The choice between hard tops and soft tops depends on factors like personal preference, budget, and desired features.</p></p>
