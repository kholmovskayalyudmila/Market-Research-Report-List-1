<p><p>The automotive System-on-Chip (SoC) chips market includes various types of chips used in vehicles. Central Processing Unit (CPU) chips act as the brain of the system, executing instructions and managing tasks. Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) chips primarily handle visual processing tasks, enhancing graphics and improving the performance of display systems. Digital Signal Processor (DSP) chips specialize in processing real-time signals, enabling audio and video functions in vehicles. Application-Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) chips are customized for specific automotive applications, providing optimized performance. Field-Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) chips offer reprogrammable capabilities, allowing flexibility in implementing various functions. "Others" category includes additional types of chips used in automotive systems.</p></p>
