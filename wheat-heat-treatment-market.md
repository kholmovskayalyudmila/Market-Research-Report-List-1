<p><p>The wheat heat treatment market can be categorized into two main types: dry treatment and wet treatment. Dry treatment involves the application of heat to wheat grains without the use of any liquid, resulting in the removal of moisture and reduction of microbial contamination. On the other hand, wet treatment involves immersing the wheat grains in hot water or steam, followed by drying, which effectively eliminates pests and pathogens. Both methods play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and safety of wheat grains, with each offering unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
