There are two main types of peristaltic pumps used: roller peristaltic pump and shoe type peristaltic pump. Roller peristaltic pumps use rotating rollers to create the squeezing action on the tubing, while shoe type peristaltic pumps use a flexible shoe to compress the tubing. Both types have their advantages, such as high accuracy, easy maintenance, and minimal contact with the pumped fluid, making them suitable for various applications in the food and beverage market.</p></p>
