<p><p>Building and construction sealants are used to seal joints and gaps in various construction projects to provide protection against air, water, noise, and dust penetration. Emulsion sealants are water-based and offer easy application and low VOC emissions. Polysulfide sealants offer excellent chemical and weather resistance. Butyl-based sealants provide superior water and air barrier properties. Finally, the "Other" segment includes various sealant types that cater to specific construction requirements.</p></p>
