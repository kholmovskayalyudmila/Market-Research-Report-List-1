<p><p>The AlGaN UV Sensor market comprises three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors detect ultraviolet light in the range of 320-400nm, primarily used for applications like curing, phototherapy, and counterfeit detection. UVB sensors measure ultraviolet light in the range of 280-320nm, offering applications in sunburn prevention, tanning beds, and sterilization. UVC sensors identify the shortest and most harmful ultraviolet light in the range of 100-280nm, crucial for germicidal purposes, air disinfection, and water purification. These sensors cater to diverse industries by providing accurate and reliable ultraviolet light detection capabilities.</p></p>
