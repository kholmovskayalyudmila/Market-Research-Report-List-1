<p><p>Zirconia powders are categorized into two types: hydrothermal method zirconia powders and precipitation method zirconia powders. Hydrothermal method involves the synthesis of zirconia powders under high-pressure conditions, resulting in higher purity and better crystallinity. Precipitation method, on the other hand, involves the precipitation of zirconia powders from a solution, providing a cost-effective and efficient production process. Both methods offer distinct advantages in terms of purity, crystallinity, and cost-effectiveness, catering to different market demands within the zirconia powders industry.</p></p>
