<p><p>Hepatitis testing diagnosis market includes various types of testing methods. Enzyme-linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA) is a commonly used technique that detects antibodies or antigens in the blood. Rapid diagnostic tests (RDT) provide quick results and are suitable for resource-poor settings. Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) amplifies and detects viral DNA or RNA. Isothermal nucleic acid amplification technology (INAAT) is a newer technique that amplifies nucleic acids at a constant temperature. Other methods may include genotyping assays or viral load testing. These different testing methods cater to different needs and preferences in hepatitis diagnosis.</p></p>
