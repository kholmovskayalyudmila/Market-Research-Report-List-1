<p><p>The 3D printing gases market includes three major types: Argon, Nitrogen, and Gas Mixtures. Argon is widely used in 3D printing processes that involve metals as it helps in reducing oxidation and improving the surface quality of the printed object. Nitrogen, on the other hand, finds applications in 3D printing of plastics and polymers, as it acts as an inert gas and prevents the material from reacting during the printing process. Gas mixtures are blends of various gases, customized to suit specific 3D printing requirements, such as improved print quality or reduced defects.</p></p>
