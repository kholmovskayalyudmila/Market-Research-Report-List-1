<p><p>The Asphalt Waterproof Coating market can be divided into two main types: Liquid Asphalt Waterproof Coating and Dry Asphalt Waterproof Coating. Liquid Asphalt Waterproof Coating is a type of coating that is applied as a liquid and dries into a flexible, waterproof seal. It is commonly used for roofs, basements, and foundations. On the other hand, Dry Asphalt Waterproof Coating is a type of coating that is applied in a powder form and then heated to create a waterproof barrier. It is often used for road surfaces and parking lots.</p></p>
