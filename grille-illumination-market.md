<p><p>The grille illumination market comprises two prevalent types: LASER and LED. LASER-based lighting systems employ advanced laser diodes to generate intense, focused beams of light. These systems offer exceptional brightness, precise control, and compact designs. On the other hand, LED-based grille illumination uses light-emitting diodes, which offer high energy efficiency, long lifespan, and versatility in color options. Both LASER and LED technologies are increasingly adopted for enhancing the aesthetic appeal and functionality of vehicle grilles, meeting the demands of modern automotive lighting requirements.</p></p>
