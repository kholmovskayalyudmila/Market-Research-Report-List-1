<p><p>The urban rail connector market consists of various types of connectors used in urban rail systems. Broad level connectors/PCB connectors enable the connection between printed circuit boards, while power connectors transmit electrical power in the rail systems. RF/HF coaxial connectors handle high-frequency signals, data connectors transmit data between rail system components, and pogo pin connectors/spring load connectors ensure reliable electrical contact. Modular and mix connectors offer flexible and customizable options for urban rail system design. These connectors are essential for ensuring smooth and efficient operation of urban rail transportation networks.</p></p>
