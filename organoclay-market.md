<p><p>The organoclay market can be classified into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based organoclays are primarily used in applications where organic solvents are present, such as in oil and gas drilling fluids and paints. These types of organoclays are differentiated based on the medium in which they are dispersed, enabling their effective utilization in various industries.</p></p>
