<p><p>Titanium Dioxide Photocatalyst is categorized into three types: Photocatalyst Solution, Photocatalyst Particle, and Photocatalyst Powder. The Photocatalyst Solution is a liquid form of titanium dioxide that is easy to apply but has limited stability. The Photocatalyst Particle is a solid form of the photocatalyst that offers decent stability and is suitable for use in water and air purification systems. Lastly, the Photocatalyst Powder is a powdered form that provides excellent stability and is commonly used in self-cleaning coatings, air filters, and other industrial applications.</p></p>
