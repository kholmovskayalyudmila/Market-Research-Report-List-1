<p><p>Fiberglass Reinforced Concrete (FRC) is categorized into different market types based on its compressive strength, measured in Megapascals (MPa). Under C30 FRC has a compressive strength below 30 MPa, making it suitable for non-structural applications. C30-C60 FRC has a compressive strength ranging from 30 to 60 MPa, making it ideal for general construction purposes. C60-C100 FRC, with a compressive strength of 60 to 100 MPa, is used for high-strength applications such as high-rise buildings or heavy-duty infrastructure. Above C100 FRC offers an even higher compressive strength, making it suitable for extreme load-bearing structures like bridges or tunnels.</p></p>
