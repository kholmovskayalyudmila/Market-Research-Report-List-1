<p><ul><li>Two Component</li><li>Three Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold cast elastomers are polyurethane-based materials that consist of two or three components, each serving a specific purpose in the process. Two-component cold cast elastomers are comprised of a pre-polymer and a curative component. The pre-polymer component provides the elasticity and flexibility, while the curative component initiates the curing process. On the other hand, three-component cold cast elastomers use a pre-polymer, a curative, and a third component, such as a curing agent or a reinforcing additive, to enhance specific properties like tear strength or resilience. Both types offer versatility and durability for various applications.</p></p>
