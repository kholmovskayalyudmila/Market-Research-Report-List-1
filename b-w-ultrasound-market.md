<p><p>B-W (black and white) ultrasound refers to the type of ultrasound imaging that displays images in shades of gray. There are two types of B-W ultrasound markets: wired and wireless. Wired B-W ultrasound systems require a physical connection between the ultrasound unit and the display device, typically using cables. On the other hand, wireless B-W ultrasound systems eliminate the need for cables by utilizing wireless technology to transmit images from the ultrasound unit to the display device, offering greater flexibility and mobility during medical examinations.</p></p>
