<p><p>The automotive cylinder head gasket market consists of various types of gaskets, including Multi-Layer Steel (MLS), Solid Copper, Composite, and Elastomeric gaskets. MLS gaskets are made of multiple layers of steel, offering high durability and resistance to extreme temperatures. Solid Copper gaskets provide excellent heat conductivity but may require re-torqueing. Composite gaskets combine different materials, such as steel and rubber, to enhance sealing capabilities. Elastomeric gaskets are made of rubber or silicone-based materials, offering good flexibility and sealing properties. All these gaskets play a crucial role in preventing leaks between the cylinder head and engine block.</p></p>
