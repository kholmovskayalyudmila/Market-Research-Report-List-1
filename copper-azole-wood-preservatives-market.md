<p><p>Copper Azole Wood Preservatives (CA-WP) are categorized into three types - CBA-A, CA-B, and CA-C. CBA-A is the most effective and commonly used type, offering superior protection against multiple wood decay fungi and termites. CA-B provides moderate protection but is mainly used in less demanding applications. CA-C offers minimal protection and is typically used in above-ground applications or areas with low decay hazard. These market types differ in terms of their efficacy and recommended usage, ensuring that wood can be effectively protected based on specific needs and conditions.</p></p>
