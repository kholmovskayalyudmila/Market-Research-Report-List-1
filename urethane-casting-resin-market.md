It is commonly used for applications that require high durability and strength, such as industrial prototypes and tooling components. On the other hand, Soft Casting Resin, when cured, creates a flexible and pliable end product. This type of resin is typically used in applications that require flexibility, such as making molds or creating prosthetic devices.</p></p>
