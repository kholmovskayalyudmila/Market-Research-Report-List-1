<p><ul><li>Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT)</li><li>Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT)</li><li>Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Nanotube Market is divided into three main types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT), and Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT). SWNTs consist of a single cylindrical layer of carbon atoms and are known for their superior electrical and mechanical properties. MWNTs, on the other hand, feature multiple concentric carbon layers, offering improved strength and electrical conductivity. DWNTs possess two concentric carbon layers and exhibit a combination of properties from both SWNTs and MWNTs. Each type has unique applications across various industries, including electronics, energy, and healthcare.</p></p>
